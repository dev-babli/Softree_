## What are SharePoint Development Services?

SharePoint development services are specialized offerings that help organizations design, develop, implement, and maintain SharePoint-based solutions. These services range from SharePoint migration and customization to custom app development, workflow automation, and integration with other Microsoft 365 services.

Most SharePoint development providers offer expertise in SharePoint Online (Office 365), SharePoint Server (on-premises), and related technologies like Power Platform, Teams, and Azure. Common examples include Microsoft Gold Partners, specialized SharePoint consultancies, and full-service digital agencies with SharePoint expertise.
