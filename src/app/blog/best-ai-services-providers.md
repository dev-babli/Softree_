## What are AI Services Providers?

AI services providers are companies or platforms that offer artificial intelligence solutions, tools, and expertise to help businesses implement AI capabilities. These services range from AI consulting and strategy development to custom AI development, machine learning model deployment, and AI-powered automation.

Most AI services providers offer a combination of pre-built AI models, custom development capabilities, and ongoing support to help organizations integrate AI into their existing workflows. Common examples include OpenAI, Google AI, Microsoft Azure AI, and specialized AI development firms.
